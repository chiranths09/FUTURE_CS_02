# Phishing Detection & Awareness Report

##  Objective

To analyze phishing emails and educate users on identifying malicious emails and preventing cyber attacks.

##  Scope

* Analysis of phishing email samples
* Identification of phishing indicators
* Awareness-focused reporting

##  Tools Used

* Email Header Analyzer
* Browser inspection tools

##  Key Findings

* Use of urgency and fear tactics
* Suspicious and misleading links
* Generic greetings and lack of personalization
* Absence of sender authenticity

##  Risk Classification

The analyzed email is classified as **Phishing (High Risk)**.

##  Prevention Guidelines

* Verify sender email address
* Avoid clicking unknown links
* Do not share sensitive information
* Report suspicious emails immediately
